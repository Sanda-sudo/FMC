About Federal Medical Center Mubi

We are committed to providing excellent healthcare services to our community with compassion and dedication.

Our Mission & Vision

Federal Medical Center Mubi is dedicated to delivering high-quality, patient-centered healthcare services through innovative medical practices, education, and research.

Our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence, compassionate care, and commitment to improving community health.

Established in 2024, our hospital has grown to become a tertiary healthcare institution serving Mubi and surrounding communities with comprehensive medical services.

We are accredited by the Nigerian Medical and Dental Council and affiliated with several medical schools for training and research purposes.
